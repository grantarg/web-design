My Color Choices

Personally, I chose this color scheme due to it's nature of a "light feeling". My favorite aspect of this color scheme is the color which I chose for the header, #385F71, and how it contrasts with the background color of the wrapper, #2b4162.

I believe that this color scheme would be used by an Ecommerce website for home goods focused on elegantly decorating a home. This would allow for the website to convey what type of type of feeling being in a home with their products feels like.
